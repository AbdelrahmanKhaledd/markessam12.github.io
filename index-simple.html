Your single source solution for all your motion control needs.

Our standard line is "custom".

Introl Design, Inc. was established in 1970, and has been providing quality motion controls and drives ever since.

Contact us for more information and you will find:

  • Introl has a broad standard product line
  • Many standard units offer a wide range of software-modified options to fit most any application.
  • We can provide you with a complete control package. We design and manufacture all of our own controllers, drives, interfaces, accessories, and supply compatible motors.
  • Windows-based interface software for set-up and configuration is provided with many controllers.
  • Customer-specific programming, modifications, and product design are available.
  • Our sine wave controllers provide smooth motion for constant torque applications, even at low speeds.
  • With 30 years of experience in the industry, and a strong customer service oriented philosophy, Introl is the right choice for your motion control needs.
  • We are a growing company looking forward to expanding our horizons. New distributors and reps are welcome.
